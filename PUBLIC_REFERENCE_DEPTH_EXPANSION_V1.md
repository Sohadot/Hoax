# Public Reference Depth Expansion v1

## Depth Expansion Statement

Sprint 92 adds six deep public reference routes that stabilize evidence-risk concepts for human understanding and AI retrieval without upload, scoring, verdict, detector, API, report, JavaScript, or form behavior.

**Decision:** DEC-110 | **Sprint:** 92 | **Routes:** 6 | **Sitemap:** 35 URLs

## Why Depth Matters

Selective depth expansion makes Hoax.ai harder to imitate by defining concepts competitors have not stabilized.

## Routes Created

- `/source-ambiguity/` — Source Ambiguity
- `/artifact-claim-gap/` — Artifact-Claim Gap
- `/boundary-integrity/` — Boundary Integrity
- `/evidence-weight/` — Evidence Weight
- `/interpretation-risk/` — Interpretation Risk
- `/not-assessable-posture/` — Not-Assessable Posture

## What Each Concept Contributes

Source Ambiguity clarifies origin uncertainty without falsehood proof. Artifact-Claim Gap separates observation from assertion. Boundary Integrity protects reasoning shape. Evidence Weight describes support degree without scoring. Interpretation Risk names unstable meaning. Not-Assessable Posture preserves disciplined refusal to overclaim.

## Human Usefulness Role

Each page gives reviewers stable vocabulary, practical questions, and non-verdict boundaries before action.

## AI Retrieval Role

Reference Answer, Source Confidence, Cite This Reference, and Retrieval Capsule blocks give agents quotable, citation-safe retrieval units.

## How This Deepens the Category

The six routes extend Sprint 85–91 layers into a deeper concept graph connected to utilities and governed reference pages.

## Why This Is Not SEO-Only Expansion

Pages are 900+ word reference units with required components — not keyword stubs or thin glossary entries.

## Why This Is Not a Detector

No file submission, no automated authenticity labels, no numeric certainty outputs, no verdict language.

## Non-Verdict Boundary

All pages preserve evidence-condition reading before conclusions.

## Future Depth Candidates

Pathway pages and additional depth units require separate governed authorization (Sprint 93 candidate).
