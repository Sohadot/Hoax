# Public Reference Strategic Surface Consolidation v1

## Strategic Surface Consolidation Statement

Sprint 99 consolidates Hoax.ai's strategic public surface across the homepage, six entry-point routes, five narrative routes, and six acquisition-readiness routes — without adding new public routes.

**Decision:** DEC-117 | **Sprint:** 99 | **Routes:** 58 (unchanged)

After acquisition-readiness pages launched, entry points, narratives, and readiness layers risked reading as separate clusters. Consolidation connects role → category thesis → strategic inspectability.
