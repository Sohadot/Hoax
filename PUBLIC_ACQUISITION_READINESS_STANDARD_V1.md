# Public Acquisition Readiness Standard v1

## Acquisition Readiness Standard Statement

Governs six public acquisition-readiness routes added in Sprint 98 (DEC-116). Pages support strategic inspectability — not transaction behavior.

## Required page structure

1. Reference summary
2. Readiness purpose
3. Strategic inspectability
4. What this page supports
5. What this page does not claim
6. Reference Answer
7. Source Confidence
8. Cite This Reference
9. Retrieval Capsule
10. Related Concepts or Related Routes
11. Boundary reminder
12. Non-transactional review boundary
13. Page-end reference navigation

## Required non-transactional language

Each page must include the Non-transactional review boundary section with safe wording: public reference-readiness page, strategic review, not a transaction page, not a pricing statement, not an acquisition term document, not a representative mandate, not legal or financial representation.

## Forbidden transaction claims

Positive or forbidden phrasing includes: for sale, asking price, valuation, term sheet, broker representation, sale offer, acquisition terms available, contact to buy, make an offer, purchase this domain, price available, listed for sale.

## Forbidden valuation claims

No market metrics, external validation authority, or financial representation on readiness routes.

## Forbidden acquisition-term claims

No acquisition term documents or representative mandates on public readiness pages.

## Human readability requirements

850+ visible words, one H1, citation-safe prose, clear section headings.

## AI readability requirements

Stable anchor IDs, Retrieval Capsules, Reference Answer blocks, internal links to registered routes.

## Citation-safe requirements

No certified truth status, declared manipulation findings, or wrongdoing conclusions.

## Retrieval capsule requirements

Primary concept, page type, canonical answer, support type, related concepts, related utilities, boundary rule.

## Source-confidence requirements

Support type, can support, cannot support, boundary, related reference.

## Boundary reminder requirements

Standard boundary text on category limits without upload behavior claims.

## Internal link requirements

Homepage link, `/acquisition-readiness/` link, 3+ utility/pathway/entry/narrative routes, 5+ reference routes, 2+ sibling readiness routes.

## Thin-page prevention rules

Minimum 850 visible words per route; required component stack on every page.

## Future acquisition-readiness rules

New readiness routes require expansion gate approval, route registry entries, and validator updates.
