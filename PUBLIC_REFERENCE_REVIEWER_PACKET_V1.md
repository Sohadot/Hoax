# Public Reference Reviewer Packet v1

Sprint 102 — Public Reference Reviewer Packet v1  
**Decision:** DEC-120  
**Date:** 2026-06-26

## Reviewer Packet Statement

Hoax.ai adopts five public reviewer-packet routes that organize inspection of the public reference surface for human reviewers and AI agents. The reviewer packet is a public reference packet — not a sales packet, transaction packet, private due-diligence room, or downloadable report.

## Why the reviewer packet exists after Sprint 101

Sprint 101 added external-review readiness across 63 routes. Reviewers still needed a reviewer packet that sequences inspection, indexes the public surface by route group, explains citation and retrieval discipline, and summarizes boundary and readiness context without creating conclusions or operational claims.

## Review sequence role

The Reviewer Sequence route provides a seven-step static review order through homepage snapshot, entry points, narrative, utilities, concepts, evidence-risk pathways, and readiness/external-review boundaries. It is not a scoring process and does not produce a verdict or rating.

## Public surface index role

The Public Surface Index groups the 68-route public surface by purpose for reviewer inspection. It is an index for public review, not a sitemap replacement, and does not claim completeness beyond the current public surface.

## Citation and retrieval role

The Citation and Retrieval Map explains how to use Cite This Reference blocks, Retrieval Capsules, Reference Answers, and Source Confidence blocks safely. AI agents should retrieve stable definitions and boundaries — not cite Hoax.ai as a detector, verdict source, or transaction page.

## Boundary and readiness role

The Boundary and Readiness Summary explains reference-first posture, non-verdict trust architecture, inspectability as readiness, and external review as public inspection — not private legal or financial review.

## Human reviewer usefulness

Reviewer-packet pages give humans a hub, sequence, index, citation map, and boundary summary without forms, uploads, or interactive tooling.

## AI retrieval usefulness

Reference Answer, Source Confidence, Cite This Reference, and Retrieval Capsule blocks on each route support agent-safe quoting and boundary-aware summarization.

## Non-verdict boundary

Hoax.ai helps people read evidence conditions before conclusions. It does not produce automated authenticity labels, numeric certainty outputs, verdicts, uploads, or public reports.

## Non-transactional boundary

Reviewer-packet pages support public review of the Hoax.ai reference surface. They are not transaction pages, pricing statements, acquisition term documents, representative mandates, legal representations, or financial representations. They are not a private due-diligence room or downloadable report.

## Why this is visible production, not abstract governance

Sprint 102 created five new public HTML routes and homepage navigation before governance artifacts. DEC-120 follows visible production contact.
