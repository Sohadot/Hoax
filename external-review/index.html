Review purpose

External review means structured public inspection of Hoax.ai as a governed evidence-risk reference system. Reviewers use this hub to learn how to walk utilities, concepts, pathways, strategic layers, and boundary pages without treating the site as a transaction page, detector product, verdict source, upload tool, or legal or financial representation.

This hub does not invite case evaluation, pricing discussion, partnership solicitation framed as commerce, or representative mandates. It teaches inspection discipline.
