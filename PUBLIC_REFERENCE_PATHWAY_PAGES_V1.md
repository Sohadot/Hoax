# Public Reference Pathway Pages v1

## Pathway Pages Statement

Sprint 93 adds six public evidence-risk pathway routes that guide humans and AI agents through Hoax.ai concepts by condition — without upload, scoring, verdict, detector, API, report, JavaScript, or form behavior.

**Decision:** DEC-111 | **Sprint:** 93 | **Routes:** 6 | **Sitemap:** 41 URLs

## Why Pathway Pages Matter

After Sprint 92 deepened the reference layer, visitors needed routes organized by evidence condition rather than generic menu structure. Pathway pages connect utilities and reference concepts into usable journeys.

## Routes Created

- `/pathways/source-unclear/` — When the Source Is Unclear
- `/pathways/provenance-weak/` — When Provenance Is Weak
- `/pathways/context-missing/` — When Context Is Missing
- `/pathways/claim-overextended/` — When the Claim Goes Beyond the Artifact
- `/pathways/traceability-incomplete/` — When Traceability Is Incomplete
- `/pathways/posture-not-assessable/` — When Evidence Is Not Assessable

## What Each Pathway Contributes

Source Unclear routes ambiguous origin reading. Provenance Weak routes custody and transformation limits. Context Missing routes stripped situational framing. Claim Overextended routes assertion beyond observation. Traceability Incomplete routes broken artifact-to-claim chains. Posture Not Assessable routes disciplined refusal to overclaim.

## Human Usability Role

Each pathway gives reviewers a condition-first entry point, practical questions, pathway steps with internal links, and non-verdict boundaries before action.

## AI Retrieval Role

Reference Answer, Source Confidence, Cite This Reference, and Retrieval Capsule blocks give agents quotable, citation-safe routing by evidence condition.

## How Pathways Connect Utility and Reference Layers

Pathways link manual utilities (Checklist, Posture Map, Synthetic Examples, Evidence-Risk Questions) to reference concepts (Source Ambiguity, Provenance Risk, Context Collapse, Traceability Gap, and others) in sequenced steps.

## Why This Is Not Generic Navigation

Pathways are organized by evidence condition — unclear source, weak provenance, missing context — not by alphabetical or hierarchical menus alone.

## Why This Is Not a Detector

No file submission, no automated authenticity labels, no numeric certainty outputs, no verdict language.

## Non-Verdict Boundary

Pathway pages teach how to move through evidence-risk concepts without producing a conclusion.

## Future Pathway Candidates

Additional pathways (compound conditions, institutional escalation frames) require separate governed authorization in future sprints.
