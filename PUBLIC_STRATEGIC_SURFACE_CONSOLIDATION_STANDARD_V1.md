# Public Strategic Surface Consolidation Standard v1

## Strategic Surface Standard Statement

Governs consolidation of the 17 strategic pages and homepage Strategic Surface Map (DEC-117). No new routes.

## Required strategic layers

Entry Points, Strategic Narrative, Strategic Readiness.

## Homepage strategic surface requirements

Strategic Surface Map, three layer explanations, three grouped link sets, three review paths.

## Strategic Surface Navigation requirements

Title Hoax.ai Strategic Surface Navigation on all 17 strategic pages with three link groups.

## Strategic Surface Capsule requirements

Page role, strategic layer, primary reader, upstream, downstream, related narrative, related readiness, boundary rule.

## Cross-layer link requirements

Entry points link to /narrative/ and /acquisition-readiness/ plus 2+ pages each from other layers.

## Strategic next step requirements

Page-specific guidance on all 17 strategic pages.

## Page-specificity requirements

No identical capsules or next steps across all pages.

## Non-verdict requirements

Standard boundary wording preserved.

## Non-transactional requirements

Safe negative language on readiness pages and homepage map.

## Forbidden regression patterns

New routes, transaction language, detector claims, template-only duplication.

## Thin-page prevention rules

Consolidation adds structure without removing required reference components.

## Future acquisition-readiness rules

Further consolidation requires gate approval without sitemap expansion.
