Readiness purpose

AI retrieval readiness is not an API product. It is the cumulative effect of page roles, stable anchors, internal links, and retrieval capsules across the public surface.

Agents should retrieve definitions, pathways, and canonical answers while preserving boundary reminders in summaries.

This page directs agents to the AI agents entry point and narrative pages that teach citation discipline.

Agents must not infer case judgments, wrongdoing conclusions, or certified truth status from Hoax.ai URLs.

Strategic inspectability

Serious reviewers inspecting AI retrieval readiness should treat Hoax.ai as governed reference infrastructure, not as a hidden product demo waiting behind marketing language.

When AI retrieval readiness is read alongside route registry discipline and publisher governance, the asset becomes easier to compare against detector-first projects that collapse evidence conditions into binary outputs.

Strategic inspectability for AI retrieval readiness depends on visible pages: utilities that teach manual reasoning, reference units that stabilize vocabulary, pathways that route by condition, and narratives that explain category ordering.
