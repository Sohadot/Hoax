Readiness purpose

Detector-first positioning is easy to market and easy to commoditize. Hoax.ai chooses reference discipline as strategic defensibility.

This page explains how refusal to issue binary authenticity labels, numeric certainty outputs, and verdict infrastructure preserves trust for humans and agents.
