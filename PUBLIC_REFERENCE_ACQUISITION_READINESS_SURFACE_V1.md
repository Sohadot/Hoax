# Public Reference Acquisition Readiness Surface v1

## Acquisition Readiness Surface Statement

Sprint 98 adds six public acquisition-readiness routes that help serious reviewers inspect Hoax.ai as a category-grade evidence-risk reference asset without transaction pages, pricing statements, or representative mandates.

**Decision:** DEC-116 | **Sprint:** 98 | **Routes:** 58 (six new)

## Why acquisition readiness means inspectability, not transaction

Acquisition readiness on Hoax.ai means strategic inspectability: reviewers can walk visible public routes, read category thesis pages, sample utilities, and trace governance discipline. Readiness does not authorize pricing statements, transaction pages, acquisition term documents, representative mandates, or legal or financial representations.

## What was built

Six static HTML readiness routes with 850+ visible words each, full readiness/reference component stacks, homepage Strategic Readiness navigation, sitemap expansion to 58 URLs, and route registry entries ROUTE-0053 through ROUTE-0058.

## Six readiness routes created

1. `/acquisition-readiness/` — Hoax.ai Acquisition Readiness hub
2. `/acquisition-readiness/category-asset/` — Category Asset Readiness
3. `/acquisition-readiness/public-reference-surface/` — Public Reference Surface Readiness
4. `/acquisition-readiness/governance-traceability/` — Governance Traceability Readiness
5. `/acquisition-readiness/ai-retrieval-readiness/` — AI Retrieval Readiness
6. `/acquisition-readiness/non-detector-moat/` — Non-Detector Moat

## How the surface supports strategic review

Readiness pages orient diligence around category asset logic, visible reference depth, governance traceability, AI retrieval structure, and non-detector strategic defensibility — all linked to existing utilities, concepts, pathways, entry points, and narratives.

## How the surface supports AI retrieval

Each page includes Reference Answer blocks, Source Confidence sections, Retrieval Capsules, stable anchors, and boundary reminders so agents can quote inspectability framing without inferring case authority.

## How the surface supports human inspection

Reviewers can sample route groups, follow internal links to utilities and concepts, and read non-transactional disclaimers that clarify what public pages do not claim.

## Why this is not a transaction page

No route solicits purchase, negotiation, or representative mandate. Public readiness is a non-transactional review surface only.

## Why this is not a pricing statement

No route states price, financial metrics, or market authority. Strategic review does not require pricing language on public reference pages.

## Why this is not a representative mandate page

Hoax.ai does not offer broker, legal, or financial representation on readiness routes.
