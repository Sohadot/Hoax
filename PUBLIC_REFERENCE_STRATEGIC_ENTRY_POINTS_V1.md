# Public Reference Strategic Entry Points v1

## Entry Points Statement

Sprint 96 adds six public strategic entry-point routes that help humans and AI agents enter Hoax.ai by role and intent without creating detector, verdict, or tool behavior.

**Decision:** DEC-114 | **Sprint:** 96 | **Routes:** 47 (six new)

## New Routes

1. `/entry-points/` — Hoax.ai Entry Points hub
2. `/entry-points/human-readers/` — For Human Readers
3. `/entry-points/ai-agents/` — For AI Agents
4. `/entry-points/research-review/` — For Research and Review
5. `/entry-points/trust-safety/` — For Trust and Safety Teams
6. `/entry-points/education-literacy/` — For Education and Literacy

## Production-First Rule

Visible HTML pages precede governance artifacts. Each route includes 800+ words and full entry-point/reference component stacks.

## Why Entry Points Matter

After Sprint 95 strengthened surface authority, Hoax.ai needed audience-specific front doors — not more concepts, but clearer entry by role for humans, agents, researchers, trust and safety teams, and educators.

## Non-Verdict Boundary

Entry points direct readers toward utilities, concepts, pathways, and boundaries. They do not produce conclusions, binary authenticity labels, numeric certainty outputs, or automated reports.
